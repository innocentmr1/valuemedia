In-Bus LED Screens (OOH)

With over 400 BRT buses plying the busiest route in Lagos, ValueMedia provides Out-of-Home (OOH) content services and advertising solutions via Digital LED screens under the brand BRT TV. We also offer specialized programmes such as BRT Fresh and BRT Cares to connect specific types of content creators to their audiences.

Exterior Bus Branding

Also known as BRT Bus Wraps or Moving Billboards; ValueMedia facilitates the sale of large print adverts on the exterior of BRT buses. We ensure efficiency and professionalism in managing all ad campaigns coming through us. We also understand the importance of brand integrity and the need to maintain & improve business relationships.

Interior Bus Branding

We offer branding on the inside of BRT buses by branding the back of the seats and top sides. This offers a relatively inexpensive and affordable means of advertising brands to the daily commuters.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Valumedia Do

We are Nigeria's top transit advertising firm. Our services include exterior and interior branding, as well as BRT-TV and RAIL-TV on buses, trains, and cabs.

How do you handle maintenance and upkeep of ad displays on transit vehicles?

Regular communication with bus operators helps us to ensure that any maintenance needs are addressed promptly. Additionally we carry out inspections for any damages or malfunctions, cleaning of the displays to ensure clear visibility, and prompt repairs as needed.

Are there any restrictions or regulations on the content of transit/Tv ads?

Yes, there are restrictions and regulations on the content of campaigns.
Inappropriate content : Campaigns must not contain obscene, offensive, inappropriate material or misleading information/claims.

Can you help with the design and creative aspects of the campaign?

Yes,we can.

Can campaigns be customized for special events or promotions?

Yes, campaigns can be customized for special events or promotions as long as they follow ARCON guidlines. This flexibility allows advertisers to tailor their messages to specific audiences or timeframes, such as promoting a sale, event, or seasonal promotion

Do you offer any discounts for long-term or bulk advertising bookings?

Yes,we do.

Can you provide examples of successful campaigns you've executed in the past?

Yes we can.

What steps do you take to ensure the safety and security of campaigns displays on transit vehicles?

Every day, our monitoring staff evaluates the campaigns running on the buses.

How do you handle any issues or concerns that arise during the course of a campaign?

We address any concerns or complaints from clients. Ensure that their needs are met and that they feel supported throughout the campaign.

Are there any addtional fees or cost beyond the quoted price for a campaign?

Asides for VATs, there are no additonal fees or cost beyond the quoted price for a campaign.
